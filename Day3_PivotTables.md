# Day 3 - Mastering PivotTables

**What I learned:**
- PivotTables let me summarize 9,800 rows of data in seconds.
- I can put Categories in Rows, Regions in Columns, and Sales in Values to see everything at once.
- Keyboard shortcut: Alt + N + V to insert a PivotTable.

**Key Findings from Superstore Data:**
- Highest performing segment: West - Furniture ($245,348.25)
- Lowest performing segment: South - Furniture ($116,531.48)
- Technology is the overall best Category with $827,455.87 in total sales

**Business Insights:**
- Double down on Furniture marketing in the West.
- Investigate why Furniture underperforms in the South.
- Protect Technology as the cash cow category.

**Why this matters:**
In 5 minutes, I answered questions that would take hours if I used formulas manually. More importantly, I identified actionable business insights.
